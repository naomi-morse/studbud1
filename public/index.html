STUD BUD

Stud Bud is a web application which helps students to stay on top of their work. It provides tools for task, time and content management. These tools will help people to stay motivated and productive whilst studying. As well as this, it allows them to never fall behind in university.









What we offer


Task management

A kanban board has been created to allow you to organise your 'to do' lists more effectively. Along with this, there is a normal task list.

Time management

A timer will help to keep track of time. Furthermore, there is a pomodoro timer which helps promote productivity through breaks.

Content management

There is a convenient reading list to help organise and keep track of all the useful resources while studying!

Content management

An embedded music player will help to maintain productivity and focus. It will also allow for more fun when working hard studying!

    Tips


  1. Make a plan and stick to it

    Plan out your time and what you would like to achieve in each study session. This will give you a clear goal to work towards.

  2. Break it up

    Assign specific tasks for each study block and hav breaks in between each session. This will make your work more digestible to complete.

  3. Create a good study space

    Choose/create an environment that works for you. Make sure you are comfortable in your chair and your desk is clear.

  4. Organisation is key

    Make sure you always stay organised to reduce last minute stress. Keep on top of your tasks by always planning ahead.

  5. Treat yourself

    Make sure you give yourself plenty of breaks and reward yourself when you are done with a task.
